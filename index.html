Kayla Robertson

Developer, designer, journalist

Hi, I'm Kayla. I'm a developer / designer / journalist based in Berlin.

I studied journalism at the University of Montana, where I found my passion for telling stories through design and technology. Currently, I'm working as a frontend developer at Buying Show GmbH.

Previously, I graduated from a full-stack web development course in Berlin, worked at the Denver Post as a design and graphics intern, managed infographics, page and web design at the Montana Kaimin and managed student designers with a focus on accessible design at the Innovation Studio. I occasionally freelance.
